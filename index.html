Visual Perception of the road lines

Visual Perception of the road lines includes to implement methods and techniques that are used in order to extract the road lines in an image that came from a camera.

My purpose with this thesis is to develop a solution that complements a great ROS software architecture with Visual Perception of road lines algorithms.
