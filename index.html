My Master's Thesis Blog

Visual Perception of the road lines

Visual Perception of the road lanes includes to implement artificial vision techniques in order to extract the road lines in an image that came from a camera.

My purpose with this thesis is to develop a solution that complements a great ROS software architecture with Visual Perception of road lanes algorithms.
